THE STORY

Decades of expeditions. Thousands of kilometers of traversing the most challenging terrain. Years of living and evolving with a machine that responds to the mountains. That's how we made the Himalayan, our first dual-purpose motorcycle.

The Scram is specially adapted to excel in the urban jungle. 19” front and 17” rear wheels help this machine take on anything that is thrown its way. A super-accessible 795mm seat height ensures rider comfort whether it’s a short hop through traffic or a longer ride beyond city limits.
